Frequently Asked Questions

Who is a ChefFood Delivery Partner?

ChefFood Delivery Partners are independent contractors, who are required to pick up food or grocery items ordered by customers from restaurants / outlets and deliver to customers. Delivery Partners use their own vehicles (Bike/Cycle) to deliver orders to ChefFood customers.

What are the login hours?

The login hours are flexible. You can choose to login anytime depending on your availability.

What are the benefits of joining as a Delivery Partner?

As a ChefFood Delivery Partner you will be provided with personal accidental life and health insurance. This provides insurance coverage against injuries and protects you against financial cost in the unfortunate event of an accident.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, ChefFood offer catering services for events of all sizes. Please contact us for more information.

What is your policy on food allergies and dietary restrictions

ChefFood take food allergies and dietary restrictions very seriously and do our best to accommodate all requests. Please let us know if you have any specific needs or concerns.

What is your policy on returns or refunds?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your order. If you have any issues with your food or service, please contact us immediately and we will do our best to make it right. We do not offer returns or refunds, but we are committed to providing a high-quality experience for every customer.
